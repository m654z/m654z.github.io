Chance

Chance is an esoteric programming language with the goal of being as hard to program in as possible.

Chance, hence its name, is heavily based on chance. Each command has a 1 in 3 percent chance of working, and every working command has a 1 in 2 percent chance of doing what you want it to do.

In fact, the Hello, world program written in Chance has a 0.000043% chance of running.
